EDH Counter is a life tracking app for EDH (Magic: The Gathering Commander) games.

Features:
• Supports 2–6 players
• Tracks life totals with tap and hold-to-repeat buttons
• Commander damage tracking per opponent (with partner commander support)
• Poison, energy, and experience counters
• Life history log per player
• Monarch and initiative tracking
• Multiple table layout options
• Starting player selector with weighted randomization
• No account required — fully offline, no tracking, no ads
